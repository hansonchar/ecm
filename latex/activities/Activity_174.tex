\begin{activity}\label{act-tableau}
\hypertarget{p-995}{}%
Insert the integers \(1, 2, \ldots, 2n\) into a 2 by \(n\) rectangle of boxes such that the entries are monotonic in rows and columns. How many ways can you do this?%
\par
\hypertarget{p-996}{}%
